Collect and organize feedback from your users

Never waste time again building features that you're not sure your users want. Track feedback from your users and build product roadmaps to make informed product decisions.

The best way to organize feedback from your users.

Wishlist allows you to collect feedback from your users, no matter where they are. All feedback will be organized in your personal dashboard, where you can track, sort, and vote on feedback. This will ensure that you're always working on the right features at the right time.

  • Users can submit and vote on feedback

    Get real product suggestions from your users. That way, you can know exactly which features they want the most.

  • Create product roadmaps

    Create public product roadmaps and keep your users in the loop about what's to come for your product.

  • Embed widgets anywhere in your site

    Wishlist gives lets you collect feedback in two ways: with your public dashboard, or with embdeddable widgets. Your users can use either to submit feedback.
